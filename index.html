what we do
Hamachi invests in technology companies that develop products and services in line with UN's Sustainable Development Goals
technologies we like
Sensors and monitoring – we’re interested in sensor technologies and environmental monitoring. Data collection is the first step towards quantifying the state of the environment – indoor and outdoor, in the air and below the surface

Analytics and visualization – cloud and big data technologies that turn data into information, using advanced visualization technologies to make the data easily accessible to all

Communication and collaboration – visual communication is fantastic for meeting more often while avoiding the negative environmental impact of travel. New real time messaging technologies drive efficiencies and engagement
